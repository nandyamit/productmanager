🤔 What is a Product Manager?

🦸‍♀️

A Product Manager is like a SUPERHERO who decides:

  • 🎯 What new apps or features to build
  • 🎨 How they should look and work
  • 👥 What people really want and need
  • 📅 When things should be ready

🕵️ They Think Like Detectives!

Product Managers ask lots of questions:

"Why do people get frustrated when shopping online?"

"How can we make Alexa understand kids better?"

"What games do families want to play together?"

Then they solve these mysteries by creating awesome products! 🔍

👨‍💻 They Work with Amazing Teams!

Product Managers are like team captains who work with:

  • 💻 Engineers (who write the code)
  • 🎨 Designers (who make things look pretty)
  • 📊 Data Scientists (who study numbers)
  • 🧪 Testers (who make sure everything works)

Together, they build the coolest tech products! 🚀

💪 What Skills Do You Need?

  • 🧠 Being curious and asking "Why?" and "How?"
  • 🎨 Being creative and thinking of new ideas
  • 🤝 Working well with different people
  • 📊 Understanding numbers and data
  • 🗣️ Explaining ideas clearly

You probably already have some of these skills! ⭐

🌟 Your Journey to Becoming a PM!

1. Stay curious about technology and how things work

2. Learn about computers, apps, and websites

3. Practice solving problems creatively

4. Study hard in school (especially math and science!)
